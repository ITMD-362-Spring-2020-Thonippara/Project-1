Support Our Community Through Your Membership

Ravenswood Manor is a strong community due to the collective efforts of our residents and Ravenswood Manor Improvement Association (RMIA). Your neighborhood association depends on your annual membership to do our work throughout the year.

  • We organize annual Easter Egg Hunt, Garage Sale, Garden Walk, 4th of July Bike Parade, and summer concert series in Ravenswood Manor Park
  • We represent community interests with 33rd Ward Alderman, City of Chicago departments: Police, Fire, Park District, local schools, local businesses, and media
  • We preserve our community parks and parkways, including LaPointe Park and our community garden
  • We support Waters Elementary School and WatersToday organization
  • We connect with residents through our free printed quarterly bulletin Manor News, email newsletters, social media, and ravenswoodmanor.com

Please complete your household membership today!

Annual membership runs from November through October and can cover any interested adult individual living in your household. But you are welcome to join or renew at any time!

We ask you to consider a contribution that is significant to you, and we hope that your will renew as a Centennial Member at $100.

We thank you for your consideration and support!
